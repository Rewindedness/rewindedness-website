Welcome!

We, Matt Bishop and Phantalsm, are the creators behind Rewindedness Studios. Our mission is to produce creative dance films that leave an impression and make you want to rewind!

Get to know us

Matt Bishop

A senior dance major at Colorado State University and President of the CSU Hip-Hop Dance Club, Matt has choreographed multiple times for CSU and has extensive dance training in hip-hop, tap, jazz, ballet, and modern. With experience as a lighting designer for CSU's summer dance program DanceLab, two years in a row, Matt has a developed skillset in lighting design and understands not just dance, but how production elements can be incorporated to support and make a dance piece better.
