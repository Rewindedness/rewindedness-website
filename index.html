We, Matt Bishop and Phantalsm, bring you Rewindedness Studios. A company that produces rewindable dance films, and we're seeking sponsors to support our first project and inital start-up.

"Who are you guys?"

Matt Bishop

A senior dance major at Colorado State University and President of the CSU Hip-Hop Dance Club, Matt has choreographed multiple times for CSU and has extensive dance training in hip-hop, tap, jazz, ballet, and modern. With experience as a lighting designer for CSU's summer dance program, DanceLab, two years in a row, Matt has a developed skillset in lighting design and understands not just dance, but how production elements can be incorporated to support and make a dance piece better.

"This is great! But what are your goals?"

Valuing quality over quantity and approaching our work with a thoughtful process, we aspire to become the #1 production company of creative dance films in all of Colorado by...

  • Leaving an impression on our audience that will make them rewind our content again and again
  • Growing to 600 followers on YouTube & Instagram 6 months after the release of our first project
  • Selling custom merch consisting of t-shirts, bucket hats, stickers, pins, socks, and sunglasses
  • Releasing "Volume 1", an album containing all the songs from our first series of projects
  • Becoming self-sustainable by the time we release "Volume 1" so that our current projects can fund future projects
